The Obella Be Bracelet

Collects vital information about your health,
allows you to track important moments in your baby's development and sends you alerts and reminders.
With a clean, elegant interface and a variety of stylish, interchangeable bands, it's as beautiful on the outside as it is on the inside.

Sensor Technology

Simple LED Display

"Obell-y Button" Light Indicator

The Obella Be App

Available online and on any smartphone, is the brains behind the Obella Be system. Record appointments, scheduled medications, important checks and the metrics you collect from your bracelet, all in one place.

Pre-Natal Data


Track your fertility, ovulation, menstrual cycle and birth control schedule to help you stay on top of your body and prepare for the most wonderful period of your life.

Post-Pregnancy Tracker


Upgrade your Obella Be App to continue monitoring your baby's growth, poop patterns, milestones, breastfeeding schedule and more.

Open Source API


Add additional applications to turn your Obella Be App into an even more robust system before, during and after your pregnancy.

Adaptable Software


Use Obella Be on any IOS, Blackberry or Android device in addition to any computer operating system.
